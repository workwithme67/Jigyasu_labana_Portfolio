About Me

I'm a passionate cybersecurity engineer and founder of JIGS.org, dedicated to building secure systems and helping organizations fortify their digital infrastructure. With expertise in vulnerability analysis, ethical hacking, and secure system architecture, I transform security challenges into opportunities for innovation.

My mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge security practices and practical implementation. I believe that security should be built into systems from day one, not bolted on as an afterthought. Through JIGS.org, I'm committed to advancing cybersecurity education and creating tools that empower developers and security professionals.

Whether it's penetrating defense systems, architecting secure backends, or educating teams on security best practices, I bring both technical excellence and strategic thinking to every project.
